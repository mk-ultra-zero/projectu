How to identify an Ultra

Being able to determine if somebody is an Ultra can be difficult. The augmentations are deliberately designed to leave little trace, and to the untrained eye an Ultra would appear similar to everyone else. It is only through our own experiences that we know what to look for, and we share that now in order that you may be able to help us find others. This guide would also be useful in helping you yourself determine if you have been modified in some way.

  • We are not claiming that every single person who exhibits one the following signs is an Ultra.
  • It is not possible to identify all Ultras via observation of external clues. Only those which have been modified with a treatment that leaves some sign. The neural interface for instance leaves none and only the individual implanted with one may be able to tell.

Personality clues

Extreme self belief
Belief in being 'the chosen one', or having a special purpose is a strong indicator that someone is an Ultra. The program designed to increase a persons self belief, requires the deployment of sophisticated psychological techniques used over an extended time period to achieve the desired effect. Making someone truly believe that they are fated to fulfill a special purpose requires resources typically only expended on those in the Ultra program. These people can sometime display extreme levels of arrogance and overconfidence in their ability, leading them to attempt things they are completely unqualified for. A musician running for president would be a good example.
Ruthlessness/Heartlessness/Insensitivity
Extreme acts of selfishness and ruthlessness can indicate that somebody has had their sense of empathy artificially impaired. For example, a person purchasing the rights to a drug which was the only treatment for a particular disease, and then increasing its price many times, thus jeopardising lives for profit, would be a behaviour that most 'normal' people would be incapable of.
Social awkwardness
Social awkwardness and other symptoms typically ascribed to those suffering from Asperger's Syndrome is a sign that a person has undergone the cognitive enhancing therapy. Often having difficulty staying present 'in the moment', becoming lost in thought in the middle of a conversation, this sign is rather easy to spot and is often a defining characteristic of the Ultra.
Mental health issues
Mental health issues have been reported by a few people we know to be Ultras. Ranging from mild cases to the more severe requiring many years of treatment. These can be caused by the treatments themselves or some of the things an Ultra would experience as part of the program. These experiences, falling completely outside the realms of what would be considered normal human experience, can be difficult to process. Those who become aware of the neural interface can also become vulnerable to mental issues, being forced to live a life where your mind is under constant surveillance puts you under unnatural stresses.

Physical clues

Youthful appearance
Ultras have been known to look far younger than their actual age. Ageing slower in comparison to siblings and cousins, is a good indication that somebody has received some kind of youth restoring treatment.
Physical strength
Ultras have been known to exhibit above average strength for their size. The biological implant which dispenses drugs is typically used for delivering steroids resulting in the Ultra developing additional muscle mass.

Other clues

Fame and fortune
Ultras are helped for a large portion of their lives to succeed in whatever profession they have chosen. Many do then go on to achieve a level of fame and fortune. Sport stars, musicians, business moguls, Ultras are everywhere, hiding in plain sight.
