Endgame

Mass manipulation

In recent years, people have begun to understand the problem of data gathered from various sources, being amalgamated into centralised database to build up a detailed picture of who they are. That understanding of them, then used to deliver highly targeted nudges to prompt them into taking certain actions, primarily the purchase of goods and services. The data could easily be put to more nefarious uses, targeting both individuals groups of people in a way that causes harm.

The neural interface is the endgame of that journey. They say data is the new oil and somebody's thoughts are the most valuable form of personal data, allowing you to target them with incredibly accurate nudges and manipulations. The neural interface itself can be used to manipulate someone directly by feeding signals into the brain, altering somebody's emotional state in a way that could make them more likely, or less likely to take certain actions. One of the more disturbing ways we have witnessed this being used was recently, when we first decided to go public with this story. One of the Ultras found themselves experiencing panic attacks whenever they tried to speak to someone about their experiences, in person or even over the phone. We believe they were induced in order to discourage them from speaking out.

Eugenics tool

Mass deployment of the neural interface would allow the implementation of sophisticated eugenics policies not possible before. A person could now be judged not only by their physical characteristics and intellect, but also by the 'quality' of their thoughts i.e. what their religious or political views are. Those deemed less fit or those who hold views which do not align with the governing authority could then be subjected to the technologically enabled abuses and persecution outlined earlier: Thought policing, sleep deprivation, induced panic attacks, dream inception, thought manipulation. The entire population becomes subjugated to a minority who control the technology. It becomes impossible to fight back against an abusive power, with the mere thought of rebellion bringing one to the attention of the authorities.

Weapon

The most worrying scenario is one where control of the technology falls or passes into the hands of persons with extreme malevolent intent. When deployed to large percentages of the population the neural interfaces become weapons of mass murder, capable of facilitating genocide. It would be naive to think that humanity is no longer capable of such evil acts especially considering the ease at which this technology would allow them to be carried out. Possibly as simple as pressing a few buttons on a command console, an instantaneous calamity could be triggered that leaves little damage to the environment or infrastructure.
