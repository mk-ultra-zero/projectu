How you can help

Find the Ultras

We stand the best chance of success by finding as many as we can and encouraging them to speak out. Something which up until now they may have been reluctant to do. What an Ultra experiences can be very difficult to describe and often those experiences are designed in such a way to make a person sound crazy if they ever decide to speak out. We need them to know that they're not, we know what they've been through, and most importantly we need them to understand that what was done to them was wrong. No matter what wealth or success this project has brought them, their lives and well-being have been put at risk without their consent. We offer a space where they can speak freely without fear of ridicule. Read our guide on How to identify an Ultra

Help prove the augmentations

Are you a scientist/researcher who has access to facilities which could help prove that a person has been artificially augmented? Perhaps you have scanning equipment capable of revealing bio implants, or perhaps you are a geneticist who would be able to tell if a person has had their DNA altered. If you believe you have a way to test for any of the augmentations listed please get in touch.

Help refine our message

We are not marketeers, we are not typical campaigners for change or have had any involvement in running other charitable endeavours. We are simply people who have obtained information about a problem that affects us all and have decided to take action. If you are someone with skills in marketing, design, copy writing who believes they can help improve our messaging, website and any other material we put out, please get in touch. If you have understood the problem we are trying to solve then you understand how critical it is that we do not fail.
